# WUOM — Pre-Conflict Observation (Early Stage)

## 0) What do you want to understand or organize?
Early-stage tensions between actors before an open conflict.

---

## 1) Modular — Elements
What parts are present?

- Actors:
  - Country A
  - Country B
  - External allies

- Resources:
  - Energy
  - Territory
  - Strategic infrastructure

- Territory:
  - Border zones
  - Disputed areas

- Population:
  - Local communities
  - Political groups

- Other relevant elements:
  - Media
  - Economic pressure

---

## 2) Matrix — Relations
How do they affect or organize between them?

- Actor A ↔ Actor B:
  - Increasing tension
  - Diplomatic friction

- Dependencies:
  - Energy supply
  - Trade routes

- Conflicts of interest:
  - Territorial claims
  - Political influence

- Alliances:
  - External support forming

---

## 3) Spherica — Context
What context surrounds or influences this?

- Historical:
  - Previous disputes
  - Unresolved agreements

- Economic:
  - Resource dependency
  - Sanctions or pressure

- Cultural:
  - Identity differences
  - Narratives

- External influence:
  - Global powers
  - Regional instability

---

## 4) Extended — Action
What can be done without forcing?

- Reduce escalation signals
- Maintain communication channels
- Avoid irreversible moves
- Observe changes in relations
- Strengthen neutral spaces

---

## 5) Reading (simple)
Tension exists but has not yet escalated.

The system shows:
- latent conflict
- growing pressure
- possible escalation if relations tighten

WUOM does not predict.
It reveals structure before rupture.
