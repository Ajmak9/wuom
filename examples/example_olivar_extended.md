# Example — Olivar (Extended)

This example shows WUOM in its extended state: flow and coexistence across geometries.

## Situation

The olive grove is not static.

It evolves continuously:
- seasons change 
- trees grow 
- biodiversity shifts 
- interventions happen 

## Layer

Extended (Flow + Coexistence)

## Structure

WUOM operates here as a dynamic system:

- Modular → local organization 
- Matrix → relational complexity 
- Spherica → holistic perception 

These layers are not separated. 
They coexist and interact.

## Flow

- nutrients circulate 
- water moves 
- energy transforms 
- information emerges through observation 

## Result

The olive grove is understood as a living system in constant transformation.

Decisions are not fixed:
- they adapt 
- they respond 
- they evolve 

## Notes

Extended is not a geometry.

It is the state where all geometries operate together.

WUOM becomes action.
